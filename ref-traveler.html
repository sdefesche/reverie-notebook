REVERIE

what you need at the table, nothing more

The loop

Say what your traveler does, asks, and notices. You may add small truths to the world at any time — a rumor, a smell, a superstition. The rules interrupt only three ways:

uncertain + costly → Crossing · nobody knows → Oracle · unexplained → Riddle

The Crossing

Name the attempt and the leading facet.
Roll Traveler d6s = facet dots, keep highest,
vs one World d6.

Higher → as you intended
Tie → Threshold Moment: succeed + owe
(keeper rolls Toll) + all look up 10s —
first real thing noticed enters the fiction
Lower → world moves; danger → mark 1 box,
or 2 if the World die won by 3+

Facets & weathering

Bone strength · endurance · steady hands
Breath nerve · charm · quickness
Dream intuition · noticing · the uncanny

Three boxes each. Full facet = closed: it can't lead, and you show it. All three closed = Long Rest — out until a real day passes. A genuine rest, earned in the fiction, erases one box, your choice.

The Oracle

1no — and it worsens
2no
3no — but a door opens
4yes — but a price shows
5yes
6yes — and it deepens

Ask aloud; the player to your left reads it first. Only ask what you truly don't know — and take the first honest reading.

Riddles — your part

  • Ask yes/no questions in turns; the oracle answers.
  • Every clear answer is written as an established fact.
  • No, and… → cross out one fact (asker's choice).
  • At seven facts, anyone may tell the truth — weave in five. Others may add one sentence each. Then it's canon.
  • Past fifteen questions, the valve opens: tell it with what stands.

Satchel

Seven slots; heavy things take two; your Burden always rides in slot 7. Full satchel? Set something down and say where — write it in the margin. Things left behind come back.

Echo-marks

When something feels charged, draw a small symbol in the margin beside it. Reuse the same symbol each time the motif recurs. Dog-ear unresolved pages. The margins are the story's memory.

Vessels — found things

find it → circle it (one sentence each)
→ roll its Charge → file it in the notebook

A charge is latent — it never fires for its carrier, only by the world's hand (solo: only on an oracle twist or a Threshold Moment).

Dreams & postcards

Dreaming (at a rest): Dream leads all crossings, nothing weathers, Tolls still bind, one thing crosses back into waking play.

Postcard received: its image opens your next passage; its question goes to the oracle; its symbol joins your margin. Tape it in.

Ways to play well

ask what places feel like · questions beat answers · running away is wise · one waymark line per passage · end on resonance, not closure

Ask what the place feels like. Write down what answers.
