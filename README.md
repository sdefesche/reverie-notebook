# Reverie — First Edition

A collaborative travel RPG of wandering, riddles, and found things, for 1–4
travelers. Played with a notebook, a pencil, two dice — and anything the world
or mind may offer.

**Start at `index.html`** (the Shelf) — it links everything.

- `notebook-game.html` — core rules
- `journeys.html` — the campaign layer
- `roads-between.html` — supplement: travel, figures, perils, postcards, dreams, openings
- `alone-on-the-road.html` / `shared-lantern.html` — solo and duo/table guides
- `ref-traveler.html` / `ref-keeper.html` — printable quick references
- `traveler-sheet.html` — interactive character sheet
- `latent-concept-spec.md` — the capture companion app (PWA lives in its own repo)
- `*-brief.md` — design briefs for the physical sheet and postcards
- `archive/` — superseded design history

Serve statically anywhere (GitHub Pages works out of the box).
